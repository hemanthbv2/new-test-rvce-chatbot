About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at RVCE offers B.E. and M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, combining strong theoretical foundations with hands-on learning. Backed by IEEE-CS, the department features a modern curriculum and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Faculty integrate active research into teaching, enriching the student experience. With over INR 1 Crore in research funding and nearly 550 publications in five years, the department is a hub of innovation and academic excellence.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at RVCE offers B.E. and M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, combining strong theoretical foundations with hands-on learning. Backed by IEEE-CS, the department features a modern curriculum and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Faculty integrate active research into teaching, enriching the student experience. With over INR 1 Crore in research funding and nearly 550 publications in five years, the department is a hub of innovation and academic excellence.

Message from

Dean cse cluster

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at RVCE continues to be a beacon of innovation, research, and academic excellence. As part of the Dean’s CSE Cluster, we are committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment that bridges cutting-edge technology with real-world problem-solving.

Key Highlights:

  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Our programmes are designed to meet global standards, with courses in AI, ML, Cyber Security, Data Science and more.
  • Research & Innovation: Faculty and students collaborate on impactful projects, publishing in top-tier conferences and journals.
  • Placements & Internships: Strong ties with leading tech companies ensure outstanding career opportunities for our graduates.
  • Workshops & Hackathons: Regular events to hone technical skills and entrepreneurial thinking.

Let’s continue to push boundaries and uphold RVCE’s legacy of excellence. 

Best Regards.
